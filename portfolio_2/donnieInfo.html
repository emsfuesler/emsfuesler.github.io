Donnie Darko Movie Poster

Donnie Darko Poster

Created in 2020 this project takes the iconic cult film Donnie Darko and creates an Art Nouveau inspired movie poster out of it. Combining the elegant aesthetic of Art Nouveau and the dark themes of Donnie Darko, the poster is meant to draw the audience into the wormhole of the Donnie Darko world. Created entirely in Adobe Illustator.
