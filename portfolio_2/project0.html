Project 000: The Helping Hands Initiative

Project 000

Transmedia world building project building off of ideas from Plato's Allegory of a Cave. This story follows a robot named Zero who was thrown away after not properly functioning and has lived in the sewers building a society from the ground up. Hoping to find a better life while still being frightened by the outside world, Zero reaches out to its internet friend (the audience). This becomes a game where the audience interacts with Zero in hope of helping it and the society overcome their fears of the outside world.
