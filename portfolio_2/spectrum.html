Spectrum 27

Spectrum 27 is a mood tracking coloring book meant to ease the troubles of COVID-19 by giving it's users an outlet for expression, self reflection, and self care. The book uses color and value to track the users mood through a month challenge of hand-drawn coloring exercises. Made with Adobe Illustator and Adobe InDesign.
