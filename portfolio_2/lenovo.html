Lenovo

Lenovo Rebranding Project

This rebranding project made in 2020 reimagines the design language of laptop company Lenovo to better compete with other tech companies like Apple and to relate more to a younger generation. The company currently employs a bland, more business associated design style. Through the "Think ___" rebranding project, we can better reach this younger audience. Made with Adobe Illustrator, Adobe Photoshop, and laptop made with Maya 3D Modeling.
