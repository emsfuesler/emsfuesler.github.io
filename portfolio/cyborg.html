Cyborg Love Story

Group project responding to Donna Haraways "Cyborg Manifesto". This is two videos showing one side from a machines POV and the POV of s girl who stumbles into the machine. In the end the become one learning that the true meaning of love is to understand one another. This was originally presented on two different tvs with two different sounds. My job on this project was setting up the room where it took place, filming, and editting the machine POV side of the video (the right video).
